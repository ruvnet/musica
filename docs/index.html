Musica VJ is a desktop app for musicians, producers and DJs. It generates a live, evolving beat in real time and lights up the screen with visuals that react to your music automatically — no timeline, no VJ software, no second laptop.

What it does

Everything you need for a live AI-powered set

No music-tech jargon required — here's what actually happens when you hit play.

🎧

An AI DJ that never repeats

Generates a live, endless beat and music bed in real time. Steer it with a genre, a mood, or 20+ ready-made styles — or just let it drift.

✨

Visuals that play the room

Eight audio-reactive visual scenes light up in sync with your set automatically. No keyframes, no rendering, no separate VJ rig.

🎛️

Perform it like an instrument

Four live macros — Motion, Atmosphere, Sculpture, Ribbon — reshape the whole look in real time, plus a full effects rack: echo, reverb, drive, crush, filter sweep.

🎚️

Plug in your own gear

Any MIDI controller, your keyboard, or the Logitech MX Creative Console maps straight to tracks, scenes and macros — no setup file to write.
